Compose compliance for any Token-2022 mint.

One meta-hook. N composable child policy programs. Every transfer passes through your allowlist, sanctions check, SNS-domain check, and any policy you bolt on — then receives an on-chain audit receipt with the per-policy verdicts.

01 · Provision

Provision your mint

One signed transaction creates your allowlist PDA, OFAC PDA, Token-2022 mint with TransferHook ext, and the ExtraAccountMetaList.

02 · Reject

Send to a stranger

Transfer to a wallet that isn't on your allowlist. The hook should reject with policy.allowlist.fail.

03 · Authorise

Add to allowlist

Single CPI to add_allowed. The MetaHook program code stays untouched — only the policy state moves.

04 · Approve

Watch the verdict land

Same transfer. Both policies stamp PASS and a MetaHookAuditEvent appears in the tx logs.

05 · Optional

Shield via Umbra

Move 100 tokens out of your public ATA into an Arcium-encrypted Umbra account. Compliance is preserved — the shield is itself a transfer, so MetaHook fires and the audit event still lands — but the post-shield balance is hidden from any block explorer.
