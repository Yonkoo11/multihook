⛓ multi-hook demo · devnet

Compose any compliance policies onto a single Token-2022 mint.

One transfer hook. N child policies. Reentrancy-safe. Audit-logged. Phase 1 demo: allowlist + OFAC sanctions, AND-aggregated.

1

Provision your Token-2022 mint

One click bootstraps everything: your allowlist PDA, your OFAC PDA (seeded with a stub sanctioned wallet), a fresh Token-2022 mint wired to the MetaHook program, and 1 000 tokens minted to your wallet.

2

Try a transfer to a non-allowlisted wallet

We generated a fresh destination wallet for the demo. It's not on your allowlist yet — the MetaHook should reject the transfer with policy.allowlist.fail.

3

Add the destination to your allowlist

Single CPI call into policy_allowlist::add_allowed. The MetaHook program code stays untouched — only the policy state moves.

4

Retry the transfer — should succeed + emit audit event

Same instruction as step 2. This time the MetaHook calls into both policies, both pass, and a MetaHookAuditEvent is emitted with the per-policy verdicts.
